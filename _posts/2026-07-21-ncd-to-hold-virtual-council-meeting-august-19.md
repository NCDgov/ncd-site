**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

July 21, 2026

**WASHINGTON--**The Members of the National Council on Disability (NCD) will hold a virtual Council meeting on Wednesday, August 19, 2026, 12-2 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time (EDT).

The event will be streamed live via Zoom for Government.

Registration is not required, but appreciated via NCD's [Eventbrite page](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/ncd-august-19-council-meeting-tickets-1993833721333?aff=oddtdtcreator).

NCD's virtual quarterly meeting will include finance and governance reports; updates from the Council Members regarding engagements with the community; a presentation and vote on NCD's disability curriculum project; a disability middle class framework presentation; and Council discussion on the middle class framework pillars before adjourning.

ASL Interpreters and CART has been arranged for this meeting and will be embedded into the Zoom platform as well as available via CART [Streamtext](https://www.streamtext.net/​player?​event=​NCD)[](https://www.streamtext.net/​player?​event=​NCD).

If you require additional accommodations, please notify Stacey Brown by sending an email to sbrown@ncd.gov as soon as possible and no later than 24 hours prior to the meeting.

Due to last-minute confirmations or cancellations, NCD may substitute items without advance public notice.

Full information on the event and any updates can also be found on our [Upcoming Events page](https://www.ncd.gov/meeting/2026-08-19-august-19-2026-council-meeting/).
